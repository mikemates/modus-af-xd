## Health Checklist

Monthly review to assess overall adoption health:

- [ ] All leading indicators are trending in the right direction
- [ ] Lagging indicators are on track to meet quarterly targets
- [ ] No unexpected churn or negative feedback
- [ ] Support tickets are declining or stable (not increasing)
- [ ] Customer NPS is stable or improving
- [ ] Expansion signals are positive (teams growing, features expanding)

**Red Flags to Watch:**
- [ ] Activation rate declining month-over-month
- [ ] DAU/WAU trending downward
- [ ] Churn rate increasing
- [ ] NPS declining >5 points
- [ ] Support tickets spiking (quality issue)
- [ ] Customer feedback becoming more negative
