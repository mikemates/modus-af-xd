## Purpose

Translate product and business intent into the user behavior, task success, interaction quality, and experience principles that must guide design, backlog, sandbox, implementation, QA, and release learning.

Product Leadership defines why the product matters. This file defines what the product experience must make true for users.

## Executive Summary

[2-4 sentences describing the intended experience and why it matters.]

Example structure:

> This experience should help [primary user] complete [task/workflow] with [speed/clarity/confidence/control]. The product succeeds when [target behavior] happens more often and [friction] is reduced. The implementation must preserve [critical experience qualities] even as scope, technology, and delivery tradeoffs change.
